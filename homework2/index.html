  • Pygmalion Project
    -I found this website while I was researching for some web inspiration for Core 1: Interaction project. This project is done by a Korean graphic designer, Cho Hyunseo. Pygmalion Project is a collection of work generated by AI based on Cho Hyunseo’s input of 10,000 images that she finds beautiful. I found the project itself very interesting. Furthermore, I liked the beauty of randomness revealed through this project.
  • Gagarin Works
    -I started to have an interest in graphic design through k-pop. Whenever any artists were releasing an album, I would carefully look at the promotion posters, album covers, and the album itself. Gagarin Works has designed a number of albums of my favorite artists.
  • Things I Have Drawn
    -I enjoy watching designs and artwork with some kind of playfulness and whimsicality. I also like adding entertaining elements in my works. Dom, the designer of Things I Have Drawn, shows both creativity and playfulness through his photoshopped images. His works are inspiring since whenever I see his works, I feel imagination turning into reality.
